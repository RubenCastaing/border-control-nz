About This Game

New Zealand has some of the world's better animal welfare laws — banning sow stalls, battery cages, and other cruel confinement systems. But there's a massive loophole: we still import products made using the exact practices we've banned.

This means NZ consumers unknowingly buy imported eggs, pork, and chicken produced in conditions that would be illegal here. Our border protects us from biosecurity risks, but not from animal cruelty.

This game was created to raise awareness about this loophole and encourage the government to ensure imports meet the same welfare standards we demand of our own farmers.
